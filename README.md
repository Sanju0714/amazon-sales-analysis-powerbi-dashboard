# Amazon Sales Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

## Overview
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard created as part of my data analytics learning journey.  
The dashboard analyzes Amazon sales data to understand revenue performance, profitability, regional trends, and customer behavior. It demonstrates how business data can be transformed into meaningful insights using data visualization and business intelligence tools.

---

## Dashboard Pages

### Executive Overview
This page provides a high-level summary of business performance through key performance indicators and trend analysis.

Key metrics displayed:
- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Total Orders
- Total Quantity Sold
- Average Customer Rating

Visualizations include:
- Revenue trend over time
- Profit trend over time
- Revenue by product category
- Revenue by region
- Revenue by payment method

### Deep Analysis
This page focuses on identifying the factors that influence sales and profitability.

Visualizations include:
- Profit by product category
- Revenue vs Profit comparison
- Rating vs Revenue analysis
- Review count vs Revenue analysis
- Discount impact on revenue and profit
- Category performance by region

## Key Highlights

- **Total Revenue:** ₹32.87M

- **Average Customer Rating:** 3.0  

---

## Key Insights

- Revenue and profit increased from **2022 to 2023**, indicating steady business growth.
- Sales contribution across **product categories is relatively balanced**, reducing dependency on a single category.
- Revenue distribution across **Asia, Europe, Middle East, and North America** shows a diversified customer base.
- **Discounts appear to increase revenue but may reduce profit margins**, highlighting the need for optimized promotional strategies.
- Customer **ratings and review counts show moderate influence on product sales performance**.
